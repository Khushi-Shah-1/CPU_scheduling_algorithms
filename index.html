How covid19 has affected people's mental health? Social media based NLP approach


We used Twitter and Reddit to find out how mental health of people was affected during Covid times.

In this project, we webscraped the data from Twitter and Reddit and I developed a LSTM model that could predict the sentiment of a post with 94% accuracy and emotions with 92% accuracy. Through Data Visualization, we derived some important city-wise insights on how mental health of people was affected. The research paper on the topic is in the process of publication.

Skills demonstrated: Web scraping, Data Wrangling, Data visualization, NLP, ML algorithms, Deep Learning
